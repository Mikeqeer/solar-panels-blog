Q1: Do solar panels work at night?
No, solar panels only work when there is sunlight. At night, you can use stored energy from batteries or electricity from the grid.

Q2: Do solar panels work on cloudy or rainy days?

Q3: How long do solar panels last?
Most solar panels last 25 to 30 years with very little maintenance.

Q4: Can solar panels run an entire house?
